Astronomy: Academic Resources

Books I have found helpful in learning astronomy

  • Astrophysics for Physicists by Arnab Rai Choudhuri
  • An Introduction to Astrophysics by Baidyanath Basu
  • Fundamental Astronomy by Hannu Karttunen, Pekka Kröger, Heikki Oja, Markku Poutanen & Karl Johan Donner
  • Hands-On Astrophysics by the AAVSO team
  • Basics of Radio Astronomy by the JPL team
  • The Physical Universe: An Introduction to Astronomy by Frank H. Shu
  • Nature’s Third Cycle: A Story of Sunspots by Arnab Rai Choudhuri
  • Astronomy by Fraknoi, A., Morrison, D., & Wolff, S. C.

These are books I believe are great and which I would like to read.

  • An Introduction to Modern Astrophysics by Bradley W. Carroll & Dale A. Ostlie
  • Understanding Variable Stars by John R. Percy
  • Exploring the X-ray Universe by Frederick D. Seward & Philip A. Charles
  • Radiative Processes in Astrophysics by Alan P. Rybicki, George B. & Lightman
