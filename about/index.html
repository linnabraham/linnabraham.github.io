About Me

I am Linn Abraham. I am basically a computer nerd, who also happens to be very passionate about Science. I am doing a Ph.D. where I work on solving problems in Astrophysics using Machine Learning. A few of the problems that I work on are:

  • Automated galaxy morphology classification using Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN).
  • Prediction of solar flares from Active Region images using Deep Learning.
  • Detection of HI sources from radio data cubes using 3D CNNs.

My work on the prediction of solar flares using Deep Learning is part of a JRF Project funded by ISRO. I developed and currently maintain the website for the Solar Ultraviolet Imaging Telescope (SUIT) along with my teammates in IUCAA.
