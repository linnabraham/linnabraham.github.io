Starting Small

This blog is built with plain HTML and CSS. No JavaScript. No frameworks. No analytics. Just words, structure, and clarity.

The entire site fits comfortably under 512 KB — enough for years of writing if you keep it simple.

You can build something like this with nothing more than a text editor and a few terminal commands.
